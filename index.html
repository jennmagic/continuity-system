Operating Protocol · For any AI project

The Continuity System

A drop-in instruction that keeps any AI project resumable and accurate — across long runs, new chat threads, or a switch to a different tool.

If it matters, it goes in a file.

The problem it solves

Long projects lose their memory in two ways

Failure 1

Hitting the wall

The AI runs out of context room and stalls mid-task, right when you've stepped away and can't intervene.

Failure 2

Fidelity loss

To save room, the AI compresses the conversation into a summary — and quietly drops details, decisions, and the reasoning behind them.

The fix isn't a smarter summary. It's moving what matters out of the conversation and into files that compression can't touch. This protocol makes the AI do that automatically.

How it works

Three small files become the project's permanent memory

PROJECT.mdnorth star

The goal, scope, rules, and where the real source materials live. Changes rarely.

Protects what & why

STATE.mdbookmark

Where we are right now and the single, literal next step. Updated constantly.

Protects where we are

DECISIONS.mdmemory of why

Every real decision, the reasoning, and what was rejected. Append-only, never rewritten.

Protects why we chose what we chose

Get the kit

    
Download it — pick your tool, nothing to rename

    
One instruction runs the whole system. Download the file named for your tool, drop it into your project, and say "Set up the continuity system for this project." The AI then creates and maintains the tracking files by itself.

Using Claude

        
Save as CLAUDE.md in your project (or add it to your existing one). Claude reads it automatically.

        
        
        
      

      

        
Using Codex

        
Save as AGENTS.md in your project. Codex reads it automatically.

        
        
        
      

    


    

      
Using plain chat instead?

      
For Claude.ai, ChatGPT, or any chat window, paste the instruction as your very first message, then describe your project.

      
      
    


    

      Optional: the blank starter files
      
You do not need these. The AI creates PROJECT.md, STATE.md, and DECISIONS.md for you on first run. They are here only if you like to pre-place empty versions yourself.

      

        
        
        
        
      

    

  


  

    
The payoff

    
Pick work back up in any new thread

    
When a thread gets long or you start fresh tomorrow, open a new one and paste this:

    

      Read PROJECT.md, STATE.md, and DECISIONS.md, then continue from the next action.
      
      
    

    
The AI rebuilds the full picture from the files — not a faded summary — and keeps going. No re-explaining, nothing lost.
